How the job runs

What you should expect on a real paint job.

1) Prep first (always)

Masking, protection, patching, sanding, caulking — this is where the final look is decided. We focus on straight lines, clean edges, and surfaces that feel even.

2) Prime where it matters

Stains, repairs, glossy surfaces, cabinets — we use the right primer so the finish coats bond and last.

3) Clean finish + clean site

We keep the site organized and leave it clean. The goal is a finish you notice — not a mess you remember.
